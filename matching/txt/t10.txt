<h2>Rajju Kootami </h2><hr>
<p>
Nakshatras are divided in to five body parts. The rajjus of the girl and boy must not be the same.</p>

<p><span class = "minahaa">Exception:</span><br>
OK, if graha maitri, rasi kootami and Tara kootami exist.</P>
<p><b>Note:</b><br>Telugu people do not give much importance to this.</p>
