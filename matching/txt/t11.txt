<h2>Sthree Deergha Kootami</h2><hr>
<p>
Sthree deergham shows how far is the boy's star from that of the girl's. Better if G -> B is 14 or more.<br><br>

<p><span class = "minahaa">Exception:</span><br>
OK, if Graha Maitri and Rasi Kootami exist.<br><br></p>
<p><b>Note:</b><br>Telugu people do not give much importance to this.</p>
