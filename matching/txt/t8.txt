<h2>Nadi - Kootami </h2><hr>
<p>
Nakshtras are divided into three groups of Nadis called - AAdi, Madhya and Anthya. Nadi indicates the health of the pair as well as the health of their future progeny.<br><br>
The Vadhoo and Vara should not belong to the same Nadi. Then, only zero points are allotted. If Nadis of Vadhoo and Vara are different, it's acceptable and gets 8 points.<br><br></p>

<p><span class = "minahaa">Exceptions:</span><br>

<b>Muhurta SIndhu - Iranganti Rangacharya:</b> <br>
1. Madhya Nadi dosha is more serious.<br>
3. Kruttika, Rohini, Mrugasira, Ardra, Pushyami, Jyeshta, Shavana, UttaraBhadra, Revati - Do not have Nadi dosha.
4. Same rasi and different nakshatra or same nakshatra and different rasi pairs do not have nadi dosha.<br>
5. There is no aadi or anthya nadi dosha for non-brahmins.<br>
6. No nadi dosha, if Janma rasi lord is Guru or SUkra or Budha.<br></p>
