Guna-Melapakam - Introduction

Melapakam or ponthana is a method of checking matching of a girl and boy for the purpose of marriage.

For matching, basically two methods are followed. 1. Guna Ponthana or Guna Melapakam based on birth stars and 2. Complete analysis of asrological charts of the girl and boy.

Please note that Kuja Dosham check is part of the second point above. Because it is easy, the first point given above is highly popular.

Guna Melanam is divided in to eight parts - Varna, vasya, Dina(tara), Yoni, Graha Maitri(Rasyaadhipa), Gana, Raasi( Bha), Naadi - Kootamis. They are sequentially given points like 1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8. Total comes to 36. 18 points out of 36 is acceptable as a good match.
