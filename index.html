I am YK Prakash ( Koti Prakash Yallapragada), an Instrumentation engineer from BITS,Pilani having 35 years of experience in the field of customer support for imported Analytical instruments used by Analytical Chemists.

My exposure to astrology started in 2003. During 2014-16, I had done a two years part-time course called Jyotish Visarada at Jyotirvasthu Vignana Samstha. But for that, my astrology is mostly self learned through books and studying charts.

The first question that crosses the mind of an Indian parent, whenever a marriage proposal springs up is - How many points?. Ashta-Kootami, a Guna Melapakam program tries to address that need. There are many such alternatives available on the net. The speciality of this program is-

  • Asks for minimal data. Just select the star and pada of the pair.
  • Works on mobile too.
  • Gives detailed split up of the eight different kootamis.
  • Explains each kootami and exceptions proposed by various experts.
  • Explains how I prefer to look at the issue - My Method.
  • Proposes a method of analysis for matching - My Method.
  • Lists suitable stars for a Girl or a Boy - My Method.

A few requests to help find the birth star and paada, given the birth details, resulted in the program called Horscope. This is not a full fledged astrology program. A great freeware alternative called Jagannatha Hora is available else where for that.
