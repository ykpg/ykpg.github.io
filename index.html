This is a free online program to calculate table of points or matching score for the purpose of matching kundali or horscopes by date of birth or by star or by nakshatra. This is widely used for vivaha pontana or marriage compatibility check or Gun Milan. You can do so in Telugu or in English.


If you do not know the star and Pada, you can use Horscope program to generate a birth chart or Jathakam or kundali of the vadhu or Bride or Vara or Bride Groom by entering date of birth etc. Then you can view birth details, Rasi & Navamasa Charts, Kuja Dosham and Vimsottari Maha Dasa & Antara Dasa.
